About Us



Introduction to Cress Jane Hospital

Cress Jane Hospital, founded in the heart of Colombo, Sri Lanka, is a leading healthcare institution renowned for its commitment to providing exceptional medical care and patient services. With a deep-rooted tradition of excellence, we serve individuals and families throughout Sri Lanka, offering world-class facilities, cutting-edge technology, and a compassionate team of professionals dedicated to improving the health and well-being of our community.

Our hospital operates across multiple locations, ensuring that quality healthcare is accessible to people in various regions of the island. From routine checkups to specialized treatments, we pride ourselves on our ability to meet the diverse healthcare needs of our patients with the utmost care and expertise.

Mission Statement

At Cress Jane Hospital, our mission is to provide compassionate, high-quality healthcare services that are accessible to all. We are committed to enhancing the health of our patients by offering comprehensive care, fostering a supportive environment, and utilizing advanced medical technologies. Our goal is not only to treat but also to empower our patients to lead healthier and happier lives. We are driven by the principles of trust, excellence, and patient-centered care.

We continually strive to exceed the expectations of our patients by offering innovative healthcare solutions, maintaining a culture of learning, and ensuring that every aspect of our services upholds the highest standards of medical practice and professionalism.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to become Sri Lanka's leading healthcare provider, renowned for our clinical expertise, compassionate care, and innovative solutions. We aim to build a healthier future for our nation by consistently pushing the boundaries of healthcare excellence. Through expanding our network of hospitals and healthcare services, we envision creating a more accessible and sustainable healthcare ecosystem, where every patient feels valued, respected, and cared for.

We aspire to be a beacon of hope in the healthcare community, inspiring trust and confidence in our services, and continuously setting new standards in patient care and medical advancements.
